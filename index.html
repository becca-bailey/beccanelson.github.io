This is my story.

People ask me sometimes why I chose to become a web developer, and I usually tell them it was an accident. I was a teacher for about five years before I caught the bug. I woke up one day, and wanted to be more in charge of my career and my life.

I started by teaching myself some HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I thought that was super fun, so I enrolled in more classes, and I am currently a student at Dev Bootcamp in Chicago. I got hooked on code, and my life has never been quite the same.
